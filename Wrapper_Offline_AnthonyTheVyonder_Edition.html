All the things you love about GoAnimate...

The Video Maker

With Wrapper: Offline's implementation of Vyond's LVM, you can turn your ideas into reality instantly, as soon as inspiration strikes. The sky's the limit!

The Character Creator

Want to bring yourself in the world of GoAnimate? With Wrapper: Offline's implementation of the character creator, you can design unique characters for your videos.

The Old Themes

Wrapper: Offline has the classic themes you've been missing. There are many to choose from, each with their own props, characters, and backdrops.

And then some!

Customizable

You can create custom character assets, props, or even your own themes! Due to Wrapper: Offline's open nature, you can do more with the LVM than ever before!

Install Anywhere

Wrapper: Offline has official builds for Windows, macOS, and Linux. You can also build the source code from scratch for other platforms.

Runs Offline

All of your video and character data is stored locally. Wrapper: Offline is also portable between systems. Download once, run anywhere.
